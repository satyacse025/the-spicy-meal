Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I receive orders from The Spicy Meal?

We recommend that you receive orders through our online The Spicy Meal for Restaurants app, which you can access from any device with an internet connection. We can also send orders to your point-of-sale (POS) system, if you use one of our POS partners. And you can receive orders via email or fax. You’ll also receive an automatic phone call within a few minutes, in case you miss any new orders we send you.
Not a The Spicy Meal restaurant partner? Sign up today to start receiving orders from The Spicy Meal!

Can I refuse or modify orders I receive from The Spicy Meal?

Yes. You can “start/stop” receiving orders or modify them through our self-service The Spicy Meal for Restaurants online portal. Our 24/7 customer service team can also assist you with live orders.
To learn more about modifying orders, please read our article How to Adjust or Update Orders from Your The Spicy Meal for Restaurants Account.

Will The Spicy Meal change our menu prices?

No. Customers value transparency, and we would never alter your prices without your explicit permission. Prices on The Spicy Meal reflect your restaurant prices.
To learn more about managing your menu on The Spicy Meal, read our article Managing Your The Spicy Meal Menu Within the The Spicy Meal for Restaurants Platform.

Can I change or update my menu on The Spicy Meal?

You can change and update your menu on The Spicy Meal at any time.
To learn more about managing your menu on The Spicy Meal, read our article Managing Your The Spicy Meal Menu Within the The Spicy Meal for Restaurants Platform.
